## LESSON STRUCTURE STANDARDS

Each lesson contains:
- **7 segments**: orientation, reading, context, analysis, themes, question, close
- **Biblical text**: Full passage in audio_script
- **Word counts**: 15-80 words per segment
- **Duration**: 6-10 minutes per lesson
- **Metadata**: difficulty, testament, book, themes, dates
